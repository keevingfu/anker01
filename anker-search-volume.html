Insights & Recommendations

Insight: Eufy's search interest is overwhelmingly dominated by 'Vacuum' products. In contrast, Soundcore is almost exclusively associated with 'Audio' products like earbuds and headphones.

Recommendation: Eufy could leverage its strong vacuum brand recognition to introduce other smart home products. Soundcore should continue to solidify its position as a leading audio brand, perhaps by highlighting premium features like ANC and Hi-Res audio in marketing campaigns.

Insights & Recommendations

Insight: Both brands show a higher volume of diverse search keywords in the Nordics compared to Poland, suggesting a more mature or varied market. Eufy has a stronger keyword presence in the Nordics than Soundcore.

Recommendation: For Poland, focus on core product marketing to build foundational brand awareness. For the Nordics, consider more niche marketing campaigns targeting specific product features (e.g., "eufy floor washer", "soundcore liberty 5") to capture long-tail search traffic.
