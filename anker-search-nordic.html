Key Insights & Strategic Recommendations

Strong Brand Recognition

48% of total search volume comes from generic "eufy" searches, indicating strong brand awareness in the Nordic market.

Recommendation: Leverage brand strength with targeted campaigns to convert brand-aware users into specific product buyers. Focus on educational content about product lines.

Security Products Dominate

Camera and security-related searches account for 65% of product-specific queries, showing clear market preference for security solutions.

Recommendation: Expand security product portfolio and create bundled offerings. Consider Nordic-specific features like extreme weather resistance.

Premium Model Opportunity

High search volume for X10 Pro Omni (720/month) indicates willingness to invest in premium solutions despite Nordic price sensitivity.

Recommendation: Position premium models with value propositions around long-term savings and superior performance in Nordic conditions.

Cross-Category Potential

Users searching for cameras also show interest in robot vacuums, suggesting opportunity for ecosystem play.

Recommendation: Create smart home bundles combining security and cleaning products. Emphasize app integration and unified control.

Model Name Confusion

Multiple variations in model searches (S1 Pro vs S330) indicate potential customer confusion about product lineup.

Recommendation: Simplify product naming convention and create clear comparison guides. Implement better SEO for model variations.

Seasonal Opportunity

Indoor camera searches suggest year-round demand, while outdoor products may have seasonal patterns in Nordic climate.

Recommendation: Time outdoor product launches for spring/summer. Focus indoor product marketing during darker winter months.
