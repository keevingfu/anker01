Key Insights & Strategic Recommendations

Poland TikTok Dominates Views

Poland TikTok generates 2.2M views (60% of total) but with only 0.58% engagement rate. High reach but low interaction quality indicates content-audience mismatch.

Recommendation: Analyze top-performing TikTok content from Norway/Sweden (1%+ engagement) and adapt successful formats for Polish audience. Focus on interactive content like challenges and Q&As.

Instagram Critical Underperformance

Instagram shows alarmingly low performance with only 126K total views across 397 posts (317 avg views/post) and minimal engagement (0.4% average).

Recommendation: Drastically reduce Instagram content by 70%. Reallocate resources to TikTok. For remaining Instagram content, focus on high-quality product showcases and user testimonials.

Nordic Markets Show Quality Engagement

Norway and Sweden TikTok achieve 1.18% and 1.05% engagement rates respectively, double Poland's rate, with additional saves and shares indicating deeper connection.

Recommendation: Study Nordic content strategy and cultural preferences. Implement Nordic-style authentic, lifestyle-focused content in Poland to improve engagement quality.

YouTube Moderate but Consistent

YouTube maintains steady 0.39-0.41% engagement across all markets with 1.1M total views. Platform shows most consistent cross-market performance.

Recommendation: Maintain YouTube investment for long-form content. Create detailed product reviews, sound comparisons, and technical deep-dives that leverage platform strengths.

Save & Share Features Underutilized

Only Nordic TikTok content generates saves (59) and shares (241), indicating higher content value perception in these markets.

Recommendation: Create shareable content formats: audio tips, lifestyle hacks, and comparison guides. Explicitly encourage saving and sharing through CTAs.

Content Volume Efficiency Paradox

Poland produces 44% of content but Nordic markets achieve better per-post metrics. More content doesn't equal better results.

Recommendation: Implement quality-first approach. Reduce Poland content volume by 30% while investing in higher production value and localization.
