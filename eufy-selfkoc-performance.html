Key Insights & Strategic Recommendations

Poland TikTok Dominance

With 55.6M views from 224 posts, Poland TikTok shows massive reach potential but low engagement (1.12%). The platform drives awareness but struggles with meaningful interactions.

Recommendation: Shift TikTok strategy from volume to quality. Create fewer, higher-quality videos focusing on viral trends and user challenges to boost engagement rates.

Norway Instagram Excellence

Norway Instagram achieves exceptional performance with 3M+ engagements from just 94 posts, showing 1.50% engagement rate and 32K average likes per post.

Recommendation: Replicate Norway Instagram's content strategy across other markets. Analyze top-performing posts and create templates for Poland and Sweden.

YouTube Underperformance

YouTube consistently shows lowest engagement (0.10-0.42%) across all markets despite significant content investment (462 total videos).

Recommendation: Reduce YouTube content frequency by 50%. Focus on high-value tutorials, unboxing videos, and long-form reviews that align with platform expectations.

Content Volume Paradox

Poland produces 58% of all content but generates lower engagement rates than Norway, which produces only 17% of content.

Recommendation: Implement quality-over-quantity approach. Reduce Poland content volume by 30% while investing in higher production values and localized storytelling.

Platform-Market Misalignment

Sweden shows balanced content distribution but mediocre performance across all platforms, suggesting strategy misalignment with local preferences.

Recommendation: Conduct market research in Sweden to understand platform preferences. Consider partnering with local influencers to improve cultural relevance.

Engagement Type Imbalance

Comments represent less than 1% of total engagement across all platforms, indicating one-way communication dominance.

Recommendation: Implement community management strategy. Use polls, questions, and user-generated content campaigns to stimulate conversations.
